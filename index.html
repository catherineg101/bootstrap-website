To the Memories

Vine

Launched on January 24, 2013, Vine was a popular platform for sharing short, six-second video clips. Despite its short-lived existence, being shut down in 2017, the platform has had a significant influence on internet and meme culture today, as well as on other social media apps.

Not only did Vine make short videos popular, but it also contributed to the most remembered memes.

The Major Impact On Viewers Like Me
Nostalgia

When I discovered this app, it was a part of my early preteen years, one of the many apps I grew up on. It made me want to delve more into creating content that was hilarious and fun for my friends to view. Because it had a short time limit, you had to come up with something quick and were forced to choose only the most funny portion of an idea or it could simply be unintentional and random. I was able to discover music and Vine also created a community where users could collaborate, especially in editing. I used to amateurly make edits during that time, and now editing communities thrive on TikTok. With TikTok, a newer social media platform that has risen in popularity in recent years, the idea is still replicated from the original foundations of Vine. Although Vine is gone, it's never forgotten.
